Kanjs (combination of Kanji and js) is a simple web app I developed to learn AngularJS. With this app, you can search through the JLPT2-5 kanji by kanji, English keyword, or Japanese keyword. You can also filter based on JLPT level. Another cool thing is you have check the checkboxes to hide a reading or kanji in case you want to study it. JLPT 1 was not included because I have yet to find the complete set. Some kanji are missing readings since they do not have any or the data I used failed to include the readings.
